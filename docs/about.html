What this is: A daily-updated, explainable “signals” ranking for S&P 500 stocks.

Inputs:

  • Prices: Trend & momentum features (20d/60d returns, MA50/MA200, RSI, volatility)
  • News: CNN + Fox News headlines via GDELT, filtered to market-relevant keywords (earnings, guidance, acquisition, FDA, SEC, upgrades/downgrades, buybacks, etc.)

What the score means: Higher scores indicate stronger recent price trend plus more positive market-relevant headlines (when present).

Disclaimer: Educational only — not financial advice. Past performance and headlines do not predict future returns.
